Business

Whether you have a small web form on your retail website with a need to automate the ever-changing data validation logic, or you are building the next big wave in machine learning or advanced artificial intelligence systems, Code Effects business rules engine can easily handle the decision making automation in your project. You can experiment with the live demo, or run one of our demo projects, or read product documentation to see this incredible technology in action.

Integration

Written in C# and implemented as a single .NET assembly, Code Effects business rules management system instantly becomes part of your project and eliminates the need to share your security context, sensitive data, and your business rules with typical external rule evaluation software. Our rules engine consists of two separate parts: the Rule Editor which is implemented as a web-based component to manage your business rules on any current or legacy .NET web platform, and the Rule Engine - which is delivered to you as .NET assembly ready to be referenced in any of your .NET projects including the latest .NET 7.0. Each of these parts can run either in perfect tandem in a single project or be parts of different projects managing and evaluating the same business rules on different machines or even on separate networks.

Philosophy

One of the main ideas behind the development of Code Effects business rules engine was the desire to eliminate the need for decision tables in business rules management, which are often difficult to work with without having IT personnel in the process. Our highly customizable Rule Editor gives end-users the ability to read and write their rules in the language of their choice without the need to learn specific Excel formats or programming languages. Our thread-safe business rules engine is capable of evaluating millions of instances of your source objects against your ruleset in just milliseconds.
